Kyu Hai {Talk2Learn} Sabse Behtar Aur Safe? 🛡️

🎓 Sirf Students Ke Liye

Ye platform strictly learners ke liye design kiya gaya hai. Yahan sabhi aapki tarah hi English seekhna chahte hain, isliye hesitation ki koi zaroorat nahi!

🔒 Safe & Secure

Humara system anonymous matching karta hai. Admin panel live activities monitor karta hai taaki platform par koi galat behaviour na ho.

Community Guidelines 📜

  • ✅ Respect Everyone: Dusre students ki izzat karein, chahe unki English tuti-futi hi kyu na ho.
  • ✅ Keep it Clean: Koi bhi abusive bhasha ya galat behaviour tolerate nahi kiya jayega (Admin can ban you).
  • ✅ Help Each Other: Agar aapko koi word aata hai jo partner ko nahi aata, toh Live Chat me likh kar unki madad karein.
